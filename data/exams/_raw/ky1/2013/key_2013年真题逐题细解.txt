II
突破范文
Wandering at a fork, a symbol of different choices—hunting for jobs, pursuing postgraduate studies, going 
abroad and starting businesses, countless college graduates are hesitating about how to make a choice.
In this picture lies an indication: life is full of possibilities and choosing the directions of our lives, which 
may determine our lives, is difficult yet important. A variety of options seem to be equally tempting owing to 
the successful cases inspiring us behind each option. Moreover, since assessing potential rewards is obviously 
impossible, making a splendid choice, as a result, is very difficult. However, starting from personal preference 
or life planning, you can make a decision more easily or perhaps move forward further on the path chosen by 
yourself. This is because personal preference is a great source of motivation to keep you moving forward, while 
life planning is the necessary guarantee of future success. Therefore, only by combining your own demand with 
different features of each option, can a rational and wise choice be made.
A sensible choice, undoubtedly, serves as a foundational factor to success. Furthermore, once a decision is 
made, you should pursue it with both endeavor and perseverance. As a college graduate, I have ever lingered on 
such a fork; fortunately and finally, I chose the path that fits my personal development: taking part in the entrance 
exams for postgraduates. Nowadays, I am following my heart, forging ahead with great motivation and enthusiasm.
